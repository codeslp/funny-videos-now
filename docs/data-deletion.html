Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 26, 2026

FunnyVideosNow does not collect, store, or process personal data from users. However, if you wish to request the deletion of any data that may be associated with your use of our app, you may do so as described below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Send an email to: brian.farish@gmail.com

Subject line: Data Deletion Request — FunnyVideosNow

Please include your name and any relevant details about the data you would like deleted.

What Happens Next

  • We will acknowledge your request within 7 days
  • Any applicable data will be deleted within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete

Additional Information

You can also remove our app's access to your Facebook account at any time by visiting your Facebook App Settings and removing FunnyVideosNow.
