In this episode of Future History, we look at three strange but entirely plausible customs from the coming decades:
• The Organ Loyalty Programs of Private Healthcare (2048): When bioprinted organs and CRISPR-edited pig parts hit the market, insurance companies turn human biology into a 3-tier loyalty program. 
• The AI Confession Booth (2041): As human priests vanish, the Vatican deploys ConfessioAI—until the Great Absolution Leak spills 2.3 million intimate secrets onto the dark web.
• The Great Pet Awakening (2026): A breakthrough in AI animal translation reveals that our pets are deeply stressed by modern life, leading directly to the collapse of the pet ownership paradigm and a wave of "pet custody" lawsuits.
