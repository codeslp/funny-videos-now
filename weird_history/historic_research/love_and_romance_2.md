**Description:** Unmarried Cherokee women would don beautiful, bright traditional regalia adorned with feathers and perform an elegant, slow "Crane Dance." *   **Visual Elements:** Single men standing in a circle watching a beautiful, colorful dance by young women wearing elaborate feather outfits, mimicking elegant cranes.
